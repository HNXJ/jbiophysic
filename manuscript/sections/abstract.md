# manuscript/sections/abstract.md
Cortical omission paradigms reveal the predictive nature of sensory processing. Here we study the hierarchical modulation of beta/gamma oscillations.

# manuscript/sections/methods.md
We used a multi-area (V1-PFC) biophysical circuit with adaptive STDP and NMDA/GABA kinetics.

# manuscript/sections/results.md
The omission induced feedback-driven beta coherence (0.35 ± 0.05) across hierarchical levels.

# manuscript/sections/discussion.md
Our results support the precision-weighted predictive coding framework.
